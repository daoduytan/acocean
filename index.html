Our workflow

With a good workflow set up and with a few tools and software, we'll easily keep the ball rolling and avoid lengthy feedback cycles.

  • MANAGING EXPECTATIONS

    Whether we are simply maintaining an existing app or designing a new app from scratch, both i and you are required to manage each other's expectations.

  • DEFINING SITE GOALS

    This is probably the most important phase of any design project as we define the goals, set up the overall structure, decide on content and assign roles and the different deliverables throughout the project. By setting up proper planning right from the beginning, we save ourself from a lot of grief later.

  • DEVELOPING APP STRUCTURE

    The app structure forms the backbone of the app. It acts as a reference guide for the team during the entire duration of the project.

  • DESIGN AND PRODUCTION

    In this phase, make sure the designer works together with the programmer to ensure the implementation of coherent design elements.

  • TESTING, COLLECTING FEEDBACK & FIXING BUGS

    Early troubleshooting saves a lot of time and effort. It is critical for all people participating in the creation of the app to be involved with testing. However, testing should be an agile process. Once we've launched the app or are in the process of launching it, a lot of people are going to start using it and will provide feedback on the app.
